Home

This hobby is working out, which covers a broad range of activities including weightlifting, powerlifting, strength training, calisthenics, cardio, etc. Not only does working out make one look bigger, but it also boosts their physical health. The beauty of working out lies in the versatility, due to the various forms of workouts that exist for various needs. In addition to this, working out is usually done in the gym where people can meet many others who share the same hobby as them. This can really allow them to connect with one another and give/receive advice.

Although it's commonly known that working out boosts physical health, its benefits on mental health are overlooked. Research shows that exercise reduces anxiety, depression, and other mental health related disorders. Working out helps people feel more positive and confident about their everyday life. All these reasons make working out a great activity that only requires around 1-2 hours during the day.

Schedule

There isn't really a set best time to workout as it depeneds on the individual and when they prefer to. This can also depend on various other factors such as their sleep and daily schedule. However, there are benefits to working out in the morning such as an increased metabolism, better mood, less packed environment, fewer distractions, etc. Benefits to working out later in the day include improved performance, a mental refresh from the day, better sleep, etc. One thing to consider here is that working out later in the day does boost sleep, but this is only if it's moderate and intense activity is avoided.

More than the time that an individual goes to the gym, it's essential that they maintain consistency in working out. This includes eating a clean diet consisting of a good balance of protein, carbs, and fats, and sleeping 7-9 hours per night. Good sources of protein are meat, eggs, nuts, cheese, protein shakes, and greek yogurt. Good sources of carbs include rice, potatoes, bread, fruits, pasta, and beans. Good sources of fats include oil, fats from meat, mixed nuts, cheese, peanut butter, and avacado.

Where

Where an individual prefers to workout depends on their own personal needs. If the goal is to lose weight, this can be done by running on a treadill or in the local park. With losing weight, the most important thing is to make sure that you're eating a clean diet and within a calorie deficit. Eating a good mix of protein, fats, and carbs qualifies as a clean diet, while avoiding excess sweet foods. Rice and bread itake should be limited since these foods are really high in carbs.

if the goals are more related to getting bigger or stronger, it might be helpful to get a gym subscription at the local gym. If you prefer working out at home, then you can even simply do calisthenics(require no equipment) or purchase equipment for a home gym. Coming to the specific gym, this can depend on the amount of equipment available as well as the membership plans and prices.

How

As a starting point, it's always important to know that no one cares how much weight you do in the gym as everyone if focused on themselves. Repping maximum weight should also not be one's goal when entering the gym. It's important to perfect the form first by doing lesser weight, and then slowly advancing to heavier weights. One important thing to know is that if one exercise is causing injuries, then DONT DO IT. It's better to switch it out with a different exercise that trains the muscles in a similar way.

We are here to help you perfect your form and determine which workouts are best for your goals. Along with us, there are many other trainers and helpful videos online that teach you how to perform an exercise the right way, which will help you grow and avoid injuries. Along with causing injuries, incorrect form also doesn't train the muscles correctly, regardless of how much or how rigorously one works out. This is another reason why perfecting form is so important in the gym.

Why

There are many reasons why someone would want to make working out a habit, whether this is to enhance their physical health or to just feel better and more confident about themselves. Regardless of what the motive is, I am always here to help you achieve your goals and there are various other resources out there.

Sometimes, working out can simply function as a healthy distraction. Regardless of the reason, working out is a form of self-care and is essential in better many aspects of someone's life.
